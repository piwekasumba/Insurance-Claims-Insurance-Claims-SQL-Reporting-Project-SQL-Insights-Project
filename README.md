### Insurance Claims Risk Analytics SQL Project

---
# Project Overview

Insurance companies use claims data to understand risk, monitor performance, and support business decisions.

In this project, I used PostgreSQL to analyse a simulated insurance claims dataset using SQL. The project focuses on exploring claims data, calculating reporting metrics, and identifying patterns that could support risk monitoring and business reporting.

The aim was to apply SQL to a realistic business scenario and demonstrate how raw data can be organised into useful information for decision-making.

---
# Business Problem

Insurance providers process large numbers of claims every day. Without structured analysis and reporting, it can be difficult to identify claim trends, monitor performance, and understand where risk is increasing.

This project demonstrates how SQL can be used to organise insurance claims data into meaningful reporting that supports business analysis.

# Business Questions Explored

This project explores business questions such as:

- Which claim severity levels generate the highest claim costs?
- How many claims fall into each severity category?
- How does claim activity change over time?
- Which KPIs provide useful insight into claims performance?
- How can SQL transform insurance claims data into business reporting?

---
# Key KPIs

- Total Claims
- Total Claim Value
- Average Claim Amount
- Claim Approval Rate
- Claim Frequency
- High-Risk Claim Ratio
- Customer Claim Activity

---
# Key Findings

The analysis showed that:

- Claim severity has a direct impact on total claim costs.
- SQL aggregation helps summarise large amounts of claims data into useful reporting.
- KPI reporting provides a clearer view of claims performance.
- Reporting trends over time helps support business monitoring.

---
# Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

- Monitor high-risk claims through regular KPI reporting.
- Review claims trends to identify changes over time.
- Use reporting metrics to support operational decision-making.
- Continue tracking key claims KPIs to improve business visibility.
